## Overview

Collably is a collaborative platform that enables experts from different fields to work
together on projects, managing every stage from research to completion. 

## Problem to Solution:

It addresses the issue of fragmented communication and disorganized project management
by offering a unified space for proposals, task assignments, and progress tracking. 

## Technologies Used

- **Figma**: Main design tool

## Challenges and Learnings

Designing an intuitive interface to accommodate various expert roles and complex
workflows was challenging, leading to valuable insights in UX design and cross-functional
collaboration. 

## Outcome

The platform improved teamwork efficiency and project transparency, resulting in smoother
delivery and better results.
