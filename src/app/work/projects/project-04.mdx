## Overview

This is an elegant e-commerce platform designed for a perfume brand. It showcases new
arrivals, bestsellers, and promotions while allowing users to browse by category and make
secure online purchases.

## Problem to Solution:

The challenge was to design a visually appealing yet functional layout that reflects the luxury
of perfumes while simplifying the shopping journey. I solved it with a clean, soft-toned UI,
intuitive navigation, and clear product displays.

## Technologies Used

- **Figma**: Main design tool


## Challenges and Learnings

Balancing aesthetic appeal with usability was key. I learned the importance of maintaining
visual harmony while optimizing for responsiveness and user flow.

## Outcome

The final design delivers a premium shopping experience, helping customers explore,
choose, and buy perfumes effortlessly across all devices.
